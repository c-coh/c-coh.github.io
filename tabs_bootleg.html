Format: Name, Symbol, health, attack, movement speed, left atk range, right atk range, above atk range, below atk range

ex: Soldier, S, 10, 1, 2, -1, 1, -1, 1


HOW TO PLAY

  • Enter in troops in either text area in any empty space, denoted by a "."
  • Uppercase letters are enemy troops, while lowercase letters are ally troops.
  • There are two basic troop types: Soldier ("S") and Archer ("A"), but you can create your own custom troops as well!
  • Note: while there are two text areas to represent ally and enemy sides, you can enter ally and enemy troops on any side you choose!
